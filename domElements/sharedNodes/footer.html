About

The reborn organization of SubByte was formed May of 2022. Project_Ra and UR_Next have been server developers for a few year by then. In fact UR_Next was a student of Project_Ra's in the early days. One day they both decided the time to create a colaboration has come. The duo took UR_Next's server brand and started reviving it. After 5 months of work they released the first APLHA version of SubByte. After that everything else was history...
